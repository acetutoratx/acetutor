Frequently Asked Questions

How do payments work?

All payments are made via Venmo. After booking, you’ll receive an email with our Venmo handle. Monthly plans are paid upfront; single sessions are due within 24 hours.

Can I switch from Standard to Personalized?

Yes! You can upgrade anytime. The $10/hr difference will apply to future sessions.

What if my child doesn’t connect with the tutor?

No problem. We’ll reassign you to another tutor at no extra cost.

Do you offer weekly packages?

No—only monthly or single-session bookings. Monthly clients get 10% off.
