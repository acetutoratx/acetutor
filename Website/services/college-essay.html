What to Expect

Our College Essay Coaching service provides comprehensive support throughout the entire essay writing process. We begin with brainstorming sessions to help students identify compelling personal stories that highlight their unique qualities. Our experienced coaches then guide students through outlining, drafting, and multiple rounds of revisions to ensure their essays are polished and impactful. We focus on helping students find their authentic voice while teaching them how to structure their essays for maximum impact. Our approach emphasizes storytelling techniques that make essays memorable and help students stand out in the competitive college admissions process.

Our Approach

  • Discovery: Uncover your unique stories and strengths
  • Development: Craft compelling narratives with clear structure
  • Refinement: Polish your writing while maintaining your authentic voice
  • Final Review: Ensure your essays are error-free and impactful

Who This Is For

This service is ideal for high school juniors and seniors who want to create standout college application essays. Whether you're a strong writer looking to refine your work or someone who struggles with writing and needs more guidance, our coaching can help. We work with students applying to a wide range of institutions, from highly selective universities to state schools. Our coaches are particularly skilled at helping students who feel they don't have a "unique" story to tell, showing them how to find the extraordinary in their everyday experiences.
